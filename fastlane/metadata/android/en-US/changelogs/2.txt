On-device transcription is now real.

* Voice notes are transcribed entirely on your device with whisper.cpp on
  Android, iOS, and macOS — no network, as always.
* The microphone inside a note now adds to that note instead of starting a
  new one.
* Fixed a WAV decoding issue that could break transcription of some recordings.

Still fully offline: no network, no telemetry, no accounts. Not yet audited.
