Optional biometric unlock.

* Opt in to unlock your vault with Android strong biometrics (fingerprint /
  face) instead of typing your passphrase every time.
* The passphrase is never stored — only the in-memory vault key is cached
  behind the biometric prompt, and passphrase unlock keeps working as before.
* The OS authentication prompt now starts automatically on each locked session.

Still fully offline: no network, no telemetry, no accounts. Not yet audited.
