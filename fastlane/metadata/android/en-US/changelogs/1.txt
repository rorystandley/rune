First public build (MVP).

* Encrypted vault: Argon2id + XChaCha20-Poly1305, only ciphertext at rest.
* Create, edit, search, and delete notes; autosave.
* Auto-lock on inactivity, lock-on-background, starts locked.
* Encrypted backup export; plaintext export behind an explicit warning.
* Local voice-note recording (on-device only).
* Fully offline: no network, no telemetry, no accounts.

Not yet audited. On-device transcription is still a clearly-labelled stub.
